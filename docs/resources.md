## HSP

- Hackathon guide: https://hsp-hackathon.hashkeymerchant.com/docs
- SDK/source: https://github.com/project-hsp/hsp

Key primitives:

- `Mandate`: payer-signed EIP-712 payment intent.
- `Receipt`: adapter-signed observation that settlement satisfied the mandate.
- `Attestation`: issuer-signed compliance fact such as KYC or sanctions.
- Verification rule: `requiredCapabilities` must be a subset of `satisfiedCapabilities`.
